pub async fn settle_auction(&mut self, nft_contract_id: &ActorId, token_id: U256)

Settles the auction.

Requirements:

  • The auction must be over.

Arguments:

  • nft_contract_id: the NFT contract address
  • token_id: the NFT id

On success auction replies MarketEvent::AuctionSettled. If no bids were made replies MarketEvent::AuctionCancelled.
